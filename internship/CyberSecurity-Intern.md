Overview
During my internship at Unified Mentor Pvt Ltd, I gained hands-on experience in cybersecurity practices related to network security, vulnerability assessment, and threat analysis. I also studied phishing, cryptography, and social engineering techniques to understand how attackers exploit human and system vulnerabilities. Additionally, I explored network and wireless security concepts and gained an introduction to post-exploitation techniques and Security Operations Center (SOC) monitoring practices.
As part of the internship, I completed several assignments and worked on documentation-based projects including SentinelShield security monitoring, malware analysis, threat intelligence aggregation, secure file transfer monitoring, and Windows service monitoring systems, which helped strengthen my practical understanding of cybersecurity operations and defensive security practices.
